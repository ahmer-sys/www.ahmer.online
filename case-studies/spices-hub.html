Spices Hub

Global B2B/B2C spice trading platform with multi-vendor support

React Node.js MongoDB Stripe

Project Overview

Spices Hub is a comprehensive e-commerce platform that connects spice producers, wholesalers, and consumers worldwide. The platform facilitates both B2B and B2C transactions, handling everything from bulk orders to retail purchases with integrated quality certification tracking.

The platform was developed to modernize the traditional spice trade industry by providing a digital marketplace with transparent quality standards and efficient logistics management.

Key Features

Multi-vendor Management

Comprehensive vendor portal with inventory management, order processing, and analytics dashboard for tracking sales and performance metrics.

Quality Certification

Integrated quality certification system with blockchain-based verification for authenticity tracking and compliance management.

International Shipping

Advanced shipping integration with multiple carriers, customs documentation generation, and real-time tracking capabilities.

Multi-currency Support

Dynamic pricing system with real-time currency conversion, bulk pricing tiers, and automated tax calculations for different regions.

Technical Implementation

Challenge: Global Scale

Required handling transactions across multiple currencies and jurisdictions while maintaining performance.

Solution:

Implemented distributed architecture with regional CDNs, automated currency conversion using external APIs, and optimized database queries for high-volume transactions.

Challenge: Quality Verification

Needed reliable system for tracking and verifying product quality certifications.

Solution:

Developed a hybrid system combining traditional database storage with blockchain verification for immutable certification records. Integrated with major certification authorities' APIs.

Challenge: Complex Pricing

Required handling multiple pricing tiers, currencies, and tax regulations.

Solution:

Created a flexible pricing engine with real-time currency conversion, dynamic tax calculation based on location, and support for volume-based discounts and special pricing agreements.
